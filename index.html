Professional Organizations

Click on each image to find out more

IEEE Professional Communication Society

This society seems to be focused on bridging the gap between engineering and writing. They hope to advance the importance and scope of technical writing and writers in the engineering community. They also exist to help mitigate communication problems between writers, editors scientists, and engineers.

Center for Plain Language

Their motto is “make it clear,” and they exist so that more language might be just that. They educate professionals about plain language, as well as critiquing their use of it. They yearly audit the use of plain language in government agencies. This can be helpful to you because they provide refreshers on plain language, as well as being a guide to how well your company or agency uses it.

Society for Technical Communications

This group serves its members by advancing the scope of technical communication in all fields. They work to promote the importance of changing complex language into easy to understand, plain language. They also help their members find the most beneficial practices and opportunities within the field of technical communication.
